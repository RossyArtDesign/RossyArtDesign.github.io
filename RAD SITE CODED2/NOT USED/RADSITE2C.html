ABOUT

Rossy Art Design is a design company owned and operated by me, Shane Rossy. I've been in the design field since 2011. By day I'm a mechanical drafter and by night I'm a husband, dad,web designer, graphic designer, and artist .

I was formally schooled as an architectural drafter in which I hold an associates degree. I have also spent years educating myself on everything design. It is my passion you could say. I have had a wide variety of clients from hearing aid specialists to universities to film production companies.

Think of me as a tool(not that kind of tool), I make your fuzzy ideas into a clear, concise reality. I strive to make my clients happy and keep them that way.
